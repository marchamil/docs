About Me

I am a student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. Currently in my first year of the Interactive Media Technology master's programme. Prior to the master's programme I did basic training in the Swedish Armed Forces as an engineer soldier. Before that, I took the Computer Science Bachelor at KTH. More often than not, I find myself in a position of management in project groups. Being able to provide creative and practical input in multiple areas of a project rather than one specific, bringing the group together, making sure people are doing what they do best and enjoy it, and making all parts of the project work together, is where I find myself most motivated and enthusiastic about a project.

What I’m good at

I am analytic, versatile and a team player. Multitasking is second nature to me, keeping track on all parts and doing what needs to be done. I enjoy variation, exploring and learning new technologies and methods. I see the strengths and weaknesses in my group

Java          C#          HTML

Organizing
